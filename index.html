STORY

About Innocent R. Utsiwegota

Innocent Rusungunuko Utsiwegota is a Zimbabwean artiste whose creative prowess transcends boundaries with a unique fusion of traditional African rhythms, modern instrumentation, and deeply poetic lyricism, Utsiwegota has carved out a distinctive niche in the global music landscape. His music is not only a celebration of his Zimbabwean roots but also a testament to his artistry and vision.

Born in 1970 in Murewa and grew up in Harare, Utsiwegota's journey into the world of music began at a tender age, inspired by the rich musical heritage of Zimbabwe. His upbringing in a culturally diverse environment provided him with a unique perspective that is reflected in his music.

Innocent's impact on the music industry extends far beyond his artistic contributions. He is revered for his dedication to preserving and promoting Zimbabwean culture through music and for his unwavering commitment to making a positive difference in the world.

MUSIC

Discography

Innocent's music not only sonically pleasing, it is also very thematic.

It's a mesmerizing blend of genres, seamlessly weaving together elements of Rock, Rumba, Reggaae, Dancehall, Traditional Zimbabwean Rhythms, Afrobeat and Soul. His songs are characterized by infectious melodies, intricate rhythms, and thought-provoking lyrics that touch on themes ranging from love and unity to social justice and cultural identity.
